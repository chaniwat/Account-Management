ACCOUNT_TYPE
----------------------------------
ACC_WALLET - wallet
    CHANGE_WALLET_INCOME - income
    CHANGE_WALLET_EAT - eat and drink
    CHANGE_WALLET_BUY - buy anything
    CHANGE_WALLET_ENTERTAINMENT - watch movie, etc
    CHANGE_WALLET_TRAVEL - money that use to go to somewhere
    CHANGE_WALLET_BILL - water, electric, etc
ACC_BANK - bank
    CHANGE_BANK_DEPOSIT - deposit money
    CHANGE_BANK_WITHDRAW - withdraw money
    CHANGE_BANK_TRANSFER_IN - transfer money
    CHANGE_BANK_TRANSFER_OUT - transfer money
    CHANGE_BANK_PAY - pay money
ACC_POT - pot
    CHANGE_POT_DEPOSIT - deposit money
    CHANGE_POT_WITHDRAW - withdraw money
EXTRA
    CHANGE_INITIATE - initiate money of that account
----------------------------------
ACCOUNT_STATUS
----------------------------------
CON - using
DIS - not use
